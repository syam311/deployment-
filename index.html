A restaurant is a public place that sells food and drinks to people. The word "restaurant" comes from the French word restaurer, which means "to restore" or "provide food for". In restaurants, food is usually served at the table by a waiter or waitress. When starting a restaurant business, the business owner must decide on the restaurant's concept, theme, interior design, cuisine, and menu. The menu, theme, interior design, and service style should be in sync. The cuisine chosen can significantly influence the restaurant's success, and can help distinguish the restaurant from competitors and attract the target market

menu

In a restaurant, the menu is a list of food and beverages offered to customers and the prices. A menu may be à la carte – which presents a list of options from which customers choose – or table d'hôte, in which case a pre-established sequence of courses is offered.
